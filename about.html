About Easysend Archive

The subsystem for archiving image documents in the IT sector of Damietta branch is one of the sub-systems of the integrated Easy Send system for managing operations that take place in the computer branch The system specializes in categorizing, storing and retrieving photocopied documents in the branch in an easy, simple and fast way All you have to do is copy the document and then do the following.

  • Entering the Document title and some related data
  • the system register the creation time and who creates the document

How to query

The system provides a means to inquire about the image documents in more than one way, and the imaged data is retrieved and viewed in an easy and accessible way.
