Easysend Archive sub-system in Damietta branch is divart of the Easy Send system for managing the branch and communircating with the affiliated missions and the central computer administration in Cairo.

This sub-system contributes to the ease of saving and retrieval of the documents that are photocopied in the branch, as they will be grouped and indexed in one place and classified by type and date of imaging, which helps in the ease of retrieval when needed.

The system is designed and developed in the IT sector of Damietta Branch

System features

The subsystem for archiving photographed documents in the branch is characterized by three main advantages: ease, simplicity and speed, where you can through simple steps photograph the required document and then specify some data such as the type of document and the purpose of its photocopying.
